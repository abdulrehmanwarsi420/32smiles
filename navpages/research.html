Recent Research in Dental Diseases


Welcome to the research section of 32 Smiles. This page highlights recent advancements in dental research, including notable studies, expert contributions, and innovative treatments. We provide detailed insights into ongoing research, influential publications, and valuable multimedia resources.

Recent Research Highlights


The Role of Genetics in Periodontal Disease


Dr. John Smith and his team from Harvard School of Dental Medicine have explored the genetic factors contributing to periodontal disease. Their study, "Genetic Predisposition to Periodontal Disease," published in the Journal of Dental Research (2023), highlights key genetic markers and their impact on disease progression. This groundbreaking research aims to personalize treatment strategies based on genetic profiles.

Read the full paper

Advancements in Non-Surgical Caries Management


Dr. Emily Johnson's research at the University of Michigan focuses on innovative non-surgical methods for caries management. Her book, "Modern Approaches to Caries Management" (2024), offers comprehensive insights into preventive and minimally invasive techniques. The book discusses new materials and technologies for effective caries management without the need for invasive procedures.

Explore the book

Antimicrobial Treatments for Oral Health


The study "Efficacy of Antimicrobial Agents in Treating Oral Infections" by Dr. Robert Lee, published in the International Journal of Oral Science (2023), evaluates the effectiveness of various antimicrobial agents in treating oral infections. This research provides insights into the selection of appropriate antimicrobial treatments to combat resistant strains of oral bacteria.

Read the full paper

New Insights into Dental Implants


Dr. Rachel Martinez from the University of California has published a significant study on the advancements in dental implant technology. Her paper, "Innovations in Dental Implants: A Comprehensive Review" (2023), highlights new materials, techniques, and outcomes of recent implant research. This work is essential for understanding the future of implantology.

Read the full paper

Books and Publications


  • Advances in Dental Research by Dr. Sarah Brown (2022) - A comprehensive review of recent developments in dental science, including new research methodologies and treatment advancements.

  • Clinical Periodontology by Dr. James Wilson (2023) - In-depth analysis of periodontal disease and treatment strategies, incorporating recent research findings and clinical trials.

  • Oral Pathology: Current Concepts by Dr. Laura White (2024) - Detailed discussion on the latest research in oral pathology, focusing on diagnostic advancements and treatment approaches.

  • Modern Endodontics by Dr. Alan Cooper (2023) - A look into the latest techniques and technologies in endodontic treatment, with a focus on minimizing patient discomfort and improving outcomes.
