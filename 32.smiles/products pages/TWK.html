Tooth Whitening Kit




Product Overview


Our Tooth Whitening Kit is designed to provide you with a professional whitening experience from the comfort of your home. This kit effectively removes stains and discoloration, giving you a brighter, whiter smile.

Key Features:


  • Advanced whitening formula for fast and effective results.

  • Easy-to-use trays that mold to the shape of your teeth.

  • Includes a desensitizing gel to minimize sensitivity.

  • Results visible in as little as one week.

How to Use:


Apply the whitening gel to the trays and place them over your teeth. Wear the trays for the recommended time, usually 30 minutes to 1 hour, then rinse your mouth thoroughly. Use the desensitizing gel as needed.
