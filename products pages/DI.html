Dental Implants


Dental implants offer a durable and aesthetically pleasing solution for replacing missing teeth. They provide a strong foundation for fixed or removable replacement teeth and are designed to blend in with your natural teeth.


Key Benefits:


  • Restores functionality for eating and speaking.

  • Maintains the natural shape of your face.

  • Prevents bone loss and preserves adjacent teeth.

  • Offers a permanent solution that requires minimal maintenance.

Procedure Overview:


  1. Initial Consultation: Assessing your oral health and determining the suitability of implants.

  2. Implant Placement: Surgical insertion of the titanium implant into the jawbone.

  3. Osseointegration: Allowing time for the implant to fuse with the bone (typically 3-6 months).

  4. Placement of Abutment: Attaching a connector piece to the implant.

  5. Placement of Crown: Fitting the final prosthetic tooth onto the abutment.

Aftercare:


  • Follow oral hygiene practices as recommended by your dentist.

  • Regular check-ups to ensure the health of the implant and surrounding tissue.

  • Avoid hard or sticky foods that could damage the implant or crown.
